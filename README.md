# hariharcostumes

Pages:
1. Main (Hero Page)
2. Costume Catalogue
3. About Us (Include reviews, FAQ)
4. Contact

> **Pages:**
> 1. Home (index.html)
> 2. Costume Catalog (catalog.html)
> 3. Previous Customers (customers.html)
> 4. About Us + FAQ (about.html)
> 5. Contact (contact.html)
> 6. One shared stylesheet (style.css)
>
> **Design Requirements:**
> - Base color scheme: shades of blue (navy, royal blue, sky blue, electric blue)
> - Very colorful, vibrant, and full of life
> - Highly interactive with animations, hover effects, smooth scrolling, and transitions
> - Fully mobile responsive
> - Modern and attractive layout, not a boring template look
> - Floating particles or confetti effects on the homepage
> - Costume category cards with flip or zoom effects on hover
> - A sticky navigation bar that changes style on scroll
> - Smooth page transitions
> - Animated counters for stats (number of costumes, happy customers, years in business)
> - A fun and energetic feel throughout
>
> **Pages Content:**
>
> *Home:*
> - Full screen hero section with animated text and a "Browse Costumes" button
> - Animated stats bar (500+ costumes, 1000+ happy customers, 10+ years)
> - Category preview grid with hover effects (Halloween, Superheroes, Historical, Fantasy, Animals, Group)
> - Seasonal spotlight banner
> - Customer testimonials carousel
> - Call to action section at the bottom
>
> *Catalog:*
> - Filter buttons by category at the top
> - Grid of costume cards with photo placeholder, name, size, and price
> - Hover zoom effect on each card
> - "Ask About This" button on each card that links to contact page
>
> *Previous Customers:*
> - Intro line at the top
> - Masonry photo gallery grid with lightbox effect on click
> - Testimonials section with star ratings and quote blocks
> - A "Share Your Story" button linking to contact page
>
> *About Us + FAQ:*
> - About section with shop story, photo placeholder, and highlights
> - Animated timeline of the business journey
> - FAQ section with accordion style dropdowns (click to expand answers)
> - FAQ questions: rental duration, deposit policy, what if it doesn't fit, extending rental, delivery, group discounts, damage policy, how to reserve
>
> *Contact:*
> - Phone number and WhatsApp button at the top
> - Booking inquiry form (name, phone, event date, costume interest, message)
> - Google Maps embed placeholder
> - Opening hours
> - Social media links
>
> **Extra Features:**
> - A floating WhatsApp button on every page
> - Back to top button
> - Smooth scroll behavior
> - Consistent header and footer across all pages
> - Fun costume emoji accents throughout
> - All placeholder images should use a colored placeholder with costume category labels
